Cleopatra was Egyptian not Greek

4/10000
100%
This sentence contains inaccuracies in its representation of historical facts.

Although Cleopatra ruled Egypt and embraced many Egyptian customs, she was ethnically Macedonian Greek. She belonged to the Ptolemaic dynasty, which was founded by Ptolemy I Soter, a general under Alexander the Great.

Reference found

Schiff, Stacy. Cleopatra: A Life. Little, Brown and Company, 2010.

This book explains how Cleopatra, despite being a native ruler of Egypt, was of Greek origin.

Detailed explanation

Although Cleopatra ruled Egypt and embraced many Egyptian customs, she was ethnically Macedonian Greek. She belonged to the Ptolemaic dynasty, which was founded by Ptolemy I Soter, a general under Alexander the Great.
